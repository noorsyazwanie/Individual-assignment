I am writing this according to my own work experience. I started working when I was 18 years old after i finished school and after Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM). I started working because i personally wanted to experience and feel on how to have your own pocket money. I worked at the restaurant just as an kitchen staff, but that small role really gave me some kind of experiences that i have never felt before. I got to gain my little knowledge about cooking, how to handle customers and many more and i persoally think that it was good to have this kind of experience because it made me more discipline.

Furthermore, the second job that i worked for just as a staff at the boutique. I worked for about 4-5 months because i needed to continue my studies in Degree. I was fortunate enough to have experienced firsthand and learned, many different sides of what goes into the general process of how something is initially planned, developed and completed, as well as how much work and detail goes into every stage. Another valuable lesson I have learned during being a boutique staff is how i handle varieties of customers and also how to become a cashier, sales girl and a promoter at the same time because it requires me to talk to the customers a lot and giving the best customer services.
